About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Interdisciplinary Forensics at Arizona State University's West Campus. My research focuses on digital forensics, with particular emphasis on data remnants analysis, anti-forensics techniques, and developing forensic frameworks for emerging technologies.

I received my Ph.D. in Information Security from Korea University in 2023, where my dissertation focused on developing a digital forensic framework for data remnants forensics. Prior to joining ASU, I served as an Assistant Professor at Sangmyung University and a Postdoctoral Researcher at Korea University.
